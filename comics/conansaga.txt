		CLASSIC CONAN/CONAN SAGA (1987): 1-97

Conan Sage was a B&W magazine that reprinted stories from Conan the Barbarian
and Savage Sword of Conan (mostly).  The title was Classic Conan for the first
three issues, then it changed to Conan Saga.

(note: there are two different comic adaptations of The Tower of the Elephant)

This summary is provided by Brian Zavitz.
